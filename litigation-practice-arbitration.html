Litigation

Kings & Alliance LLP is a renowned law firm and holds expertise in providing highly superior quality credible Litigation Services to our clients across different industries and business sectors having business operations inside and outside India. Our team of best & professional lawyers have considerable knowledge and experience to contest litigation on behalf of our clients and has been providing assistance in litigation in all Civil, Criminal, Corporate & IPR matters.

Litigation is not simply another name for a lawsuit. Litigation includes any number of activities before, during, and after a lawsuit to enforce a legal right. In addition to the actual lawsuit, pre-suit negotiations, arbitrations, facilitations and appeals may also be part of the litigation process.

Our Objective

  • To engage in pre-litigation settlement discussions to resolve the matter before a lawsuit is filed.
  • To assist, advise and represent clients before Supreme Court, Various High Courts & District Courts and all other Tribunals, Boards & Local Judicial Authorities across and outside India.
  • To represent plaintiffs and defendants in all matters and manage all phases of the litigation process.
